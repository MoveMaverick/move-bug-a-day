## **The seed for minting does not update**

**Vulnerability Pattern:** Randomness Issue

**Severity:** Low

**Ecosystem:** Sui

**Protocol:** MystenLabs Sui

**Auditor:** OtterSec

**Report:** 

[https://ottersec.notion.site/Sampled-Public-Audit-Reports-a296e98838aa4fdb8f3b192663400772](https://www.notion.so/Sampled-Public-Audit-Reports-a296e98838aa4fdb8f3b192663400772?pvs=21)

**Report Date:** May 2023

**Description:** This field should update after every mint with a new value

**Recommendation:** Set the self.inner_hash value to genes at the end of mint.

**Coding/Auditing Tip:** Ensure hashes are randomized, updated, and not predictable for front-running attacks.
